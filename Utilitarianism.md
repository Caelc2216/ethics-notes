# Utilitarianism
- Maximize Utility -> Greatest Good -> Happiness/pleasure/joy
- British Empiricism


## Jeremy Bentham
- Hedonistic calculus: intensity, duration, certainty, propinquity (how far away), fecundity (fertility), purity (does it bring about the opposite effect), extent (reach)
- Focuses on Quantity

## John Stewart Mill
- Quantity + Quality
- Development of higher faculties
- Ask those acquainted with both

### Mill's 4 responses
- Hedonistic Doctrine
- Godless doctrine
- No time
- Ends justify the means
